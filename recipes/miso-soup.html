Miso Soup

Description

The Miso soup is a soup full of savory flavors.

It's a traditional japanese soup, composed of miso pase (japanese seasoning produced by fermenting beans or grains) and other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 teaspoons of dashi granules
  • 3 tablespoons of miso paste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package of diced silken tofu
  • 2 green onions, sliced diagonally into 1⁄2 inch pieces

Steps

  1. Combine water and dashi granules into a medium saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and whisk in the miso paste.
  4. Stir in the tofu.
  5. Separate the layers of green onions.
  6. Add them to the soup.
  7. Simmer gently for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
  8. Enjoy your meal!
