Caesar Salad

Description

Green mexican salad.

Caesar Salad is a green salad primarily composed of romain lettuce. According to legend, it was created on July 4, 1924, by Caesar Cardini at his place, viz. Caesar's, a restaurant inside a hotel, after the kitchen was overwhelmed and he had to quickly cobble a dish together with the ingredients he had.

Ingredients

  • 1⁄2 juiced lemon
  • 1⁄4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons of red wine vinegar
  • 1 dash of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 large egg yol
  • 2 cloves of finely chopped garlic
  • 3 anchovy fillets
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Make the dressing.
    1. Mash anchovy fillets and garlic in a salad bowl.
    2. Add lemon juice.
    3. Add red wine vinegar.
    4. Add Dijon mustard.
    5. Add egg yolk.
    6. Add Worcestershire sauce.
    7. Whisk until it is smooth and creamy.
    8. Gradually steam in olive oil while whisking constantly.
    9. Season with salt and black pepper.
  2. Make the salad.
    1. Gently mix romaine lettuce and Parmesan cheese into dressing until thoroughly coated.
  3. Add croutons as toppings to the salad.
  4. Enjoy your meal!
