Caesar Salad

Description

Green mexican salad.

Caesar Salad is a green salad primarily composed of romain lettuce. According to legend, it was created on July 4, 1924, by Caesar Cardini at his place, viz. Caesar's, a restaurant inside a hotel, after the kitchen was overwhelmed and he had to quickly cobble a dish together with the ingredients he had.

Ingredients

  • 1⁄2 juiced lemon
  • 1⁄4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons of red wine vinegar
  • 1 dash of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 large egg yol
  • 2 cloves of finely chopped garlic
  • 3 anchovy fillets
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
