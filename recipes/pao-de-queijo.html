Pão de Queijo

Description

Pão de queijo is a gluten and wheat-free cheese bread.

The name is in portuguese because it is thought to be the characteristic dish of the brazilian state of Minas Gerais - but, apparently, it is also present in other Latin American cuisines.

Ingredients

  • 1⁄3 cup of water
  • 1⁄2 cup of olive oil or butter
  • 2⁄3 cup of freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of minced garlic
  • 2 cups of tapioca flour
  • 2 beaten eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 190 °C (375 °F).
  2. Mix the olive oil, water, milk and salt in a large saucepan and place place it in the oven.
  3. Bring to a boil and immediately remove from the heat.
  4. Stir in tapioca flour and garlic, stirring until smooth.
  5. Set aside to rest for 10 to 15 minutes.
  6. Stir cheese and eggs into the tapioca mixture until combined. The end result should be a chunky dough.
  7. Drop dough by 1⁄4 cup-size balls onto an ungreased baking sheet.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until the tops are lightly browned. This should take from 15–20 minutes.
  9. Enjoy your meal!
